Parmesan Crusted Chicken


Home

This is aa parmesan crusted chicken brest. Total time needed is around 40 minutes


Ingredient List:

  • 1.5 pounds of chicken breast
  • Olive oil
  • Salted Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Italian Style Panko
  • Flour
  • 2 eggs
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Butterfly and split each chicken breasts into two portions, making four pieces total. Cover chicken with plastic wrap and pound each piece to an even 1/4-inch thickness with the flat side of a meat mallet.
  2. In one shallow dish add flour, Italian seasoning and garlic powder and whisk to blend.
  3. In a second shallow dish whisk eggs together until blended.
  4. In a third shallow dish toss together panko and parmesan.
  5. Season chicken cutlets with salt and pepper on each side. Then working with one at a time dredge both sides in flour mixture, transfer to eggs and dredge both sides, lift and let excess run of. Transfer to panko mixture, coat both sides while pressing mixture in to coat well.
  6. Heat 1/4 cup olive in a 12-inch heavy non-stick skillet over medium heat.

Pan Seared Ribeye Steak


Home

This is an easy recipe for a pan seared ribeye steak. Total time needed is around 40 minutes


Ingredient List:

  • 1 8 oz ribeye steak
  • Olive oil
  • Salted Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 3 garlic cloves

Steps:

  1. Let steak sit at room temperature for 20 minutes
  2. Heat medium stainless steel or cast iron pan until hot
  3. Season steak generously with salt and pepper
  4. Add olive oil in the pan, let the oil heat for about 20 seconds
  5. Add steak to pan, sear for 1 minute and flip the steak. Do this every minute until steak is done
  6. Crush the garlic cloves and add to pan
  7. Before the steak is done, add butter and baste for 15 seconds on each side
  8. Remove steak and cover with butter/oil that is still in the pan
  9. Let steak rest for 3 minutes before serving
